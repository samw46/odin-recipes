Sam's Terrific Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Turkey Breast
  • Bread (Whatever bread you like, I am a sourdough guy myself)
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Duke's Mayo or Kewpie Mayo are acceptable mayos
  • Tomato

Steps of Creation

  1. Toast your bread using your preferred method of toasting
  2. Place your sliced turkey breast a toasted bread slice
  3. Place your lettuce on top of your turkey breast.
  4. Place your tomato on top of your lettuce.
  5. Spread you Mayo on the other slice of toast.
  6. Combine your two slices to make a beautiful, sexy, scrumptious sandwich.
