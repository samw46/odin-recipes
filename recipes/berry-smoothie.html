Sam's Bombastic Berry Smoothie

The above picture is a picture of a Strawberry Banana Smoothie. This is not a smoothie that I personally created, just a random one I found on the internet. My smoothies are not that pretty.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Raw Spinach
  • 1 Cup of Milk (Any kind of milk will work here, I prefer Oat Milk, but I have used Almond and 2% and it always tastes great.)
  • 1 Cup Frozen Berry Medley (I get mine from Aldi, but whatever bag you chose from your local supermarket will work fine.)
  • Half a Banana

How to Make:

  1. Measure out your Raw Spinach and add to blender.
  2. Measure out your milk and add to blender.
  3. Blend milk and spinach until smooth.
  4. Add your berries and bananas to the blender.
  5. Blend until smooth.
  6. Enjoy!
