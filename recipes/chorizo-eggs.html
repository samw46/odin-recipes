Sam's Carnivorous Chorizo and Eggs

This is an image of Chorizo and Eggs, I think mine are better. I will tell you how to make them now.

Ingredients

  • 1 Serving Chorizo
  • Half a Green or Red Bell Pepper (or Both!)
  • 1/4 of a Spanish Onion
  • 2 Eggs

Steps of Creation

  1. Heat up a medium sized skillet over Medium High heat
  2. Dice your Peppers and Onions and add them to the skillet along with a half tablespoon of oil.
  3. Once your onions and peppers have softened and are starting to brown, add in your chorizo.
  4. Break up your chorizo and brown it along the onions and peppers.
  5. Create your two spaces for you to crack your egss.
  6. Crack your eggs into your spaces and immediately break the yolk and mix everything together until your eggs have cooked through.
  7. Add to plate and eat in 20 seconds. (I like to eat this with a tortilla. Either making a taco or breaking the tortilla off to use as a spoon.)
